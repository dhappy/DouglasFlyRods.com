All of my rods are hand made from Tokin bamboo and are available for sale in custom lengths, weights, and wrap colors. Though some may wish to put their bamboo rod on display, I make each one to be taken to the river. For this reason, each two-piece rod comes with two duplicate tip sections.

I use the highest quality hardware and materials including: nickel silver ferrules, Recoil guides, 100% silk wraps, nickel silver or anodized aluminum reels seats, flor grade cork handles, and polyurethene varnish.

Recommended line weights by length are: 6ft-3wt, 7ft-4wt, 7.5ft-5wt, 8ft-6wt/7wt, 9ft-9wt.

Each rod comes with an aluminum tube case for no additional charge.
